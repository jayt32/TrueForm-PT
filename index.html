Conditions & Focus Areas

What we treat

Post-Operative Rehab

Structured recovery following orthopedic surgeries — knee, shoulder, spinal procedures, and joint replacements. Goal-driven progressions from early mobility to full return to function.

Chronic Pain & Movement Optimization

Addressing persistent musculoskeletal pain through targeted therapy, movement retraining, and strategies that restore confidence in daily activity.

Balance & Fall Prevention

Stability training, proprioceptive rehabilitation, and individualized programming for adults looking to improve confidence, reduce fall risk, and stay active.

Return-to-Function Programming

For patients whose recovery has stalled or who need a more personalized approach. Sessions built around your specific goals — whether that's returning to work, sport, or everyday life.

Meet Your Therapist

Alisha Trivedi

Doctor of Physical Therapy

I earned my DPT from Nova Southeastern and went on to practice at Good Shepherd Penn Partners, an outpatient clinic in affiliation with Penn Medicine. There I worked alongside experienced clinicians treating a wide range of orthopedic and musculoskeletal conditions — post-operative rehab, chronic pain, mobility limitations, and day-to-day functional impairments — with a consistent focus on reducing pain, restoring movement, and helping patients get back to what matters to them.

After completing my orthopedic residency at Arcadia University, I made my way to Tampa and started TrueForm to practice the way I've always believed PT should be done: unhurried, one-on-one, and centered around you.
